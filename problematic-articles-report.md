- ⚠️ **No specific author** - Listed as "Editorial Team" or no author
- ⚠️ **Short content** - 300-500 words (thin content)
- ⚠️ **No executive quotes** - Missing interview elements
- ⚠️ **Generic business advice** - Could be found on any business blog

- Specific company examples
- Real metrics/results

✅ **Make it Longer**
- Target: 800-1500 words minimum
- Add depth, context, real-world examples

✅ **Assign Specific Author**
- Staff writer name
- Contributing editor
- Not "Editorial Team"

✅ **Include Visuals**
- Executive headshot
- Company photos
- Infographics if relevant
